          Welcome to Pokemon Quick RPG

          Instruction

          You'll begin the game choosing from among 4 different pokemon. Once chosen you will have to face the remaining 3. If you manage to survive and defeat all 3 pokemon, victory is yours!

          Note that you have potions and ethers to assist you in battle!

          • Potions will heal you for 80%-100% of the pokemon's max HP.
          • Ethers will restore 5 PP to all abilities.
